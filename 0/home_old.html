Welcome to the CANREST
Research Study

We invite you to participate in an exciting new research study to determine the effect of a potential new cannabis medication in reducing insomnia.

You may be elibgible to participate if you:

Are aged 18 to 65 years
Currently live in Australia
Having trouble staying and falling asleep
Have internet and phone access

If eligible you will be required to take the cannabidiol or placebo (inactive capsule) for 8 weeks, complete questionnaires and wear a wristwatch that measures your sleep patterns for one week before treatment and for one week towards the end of treatment. We will also require a fasting blood and urine sample before and towards the end of the treatment period. If you are eligible and participate in the study, you will receive renumeration for your time and there will be no out of pocket expense to you.

If you are interested to learn more and find out if this study is suitable for you, please complete the screening questionnaire by answering the following statement:

I agree to complete a screening questionnaire to find out if this study is suitable for me:

Completing questionnaires is necessary to be part of the study.

This study has received ethical approval from Bellberry Human Research Ethics Committee (ref: HREC2021-09-1049) and is sponsored by BOD Ltd.
